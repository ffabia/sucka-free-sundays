A Very Short Story

So, my father named me Frances, after Francis Scott Bacon, who made very moving, some would even say creepy paintings in the early 19th century. His last name, which I also inherited, is Fabian. One day on the first day of school, our teacher asked us put our initials on our cubbies, put our things away and introduce ourselves. Naturally, I wrote "F.F." and before I could say my name, a set of twins yelled out "french fry!"



*Cue eye roll to back of head*

Little did they know, I loved french fries. I'd dip them in my frosty or milkshake, eat them in sandwhiches...Love.I could even recall saying to myself french fry is a friggen awesome name.


So there you have it- a very short story about how I've always known people will try to kill my vibe, but never truely can.
